In this weeks "What's New?" we're going medevil with 2 medevil themed games!


First off is the long awaited Eldins Ring, an action roleplaying game made by the legendary FROMSoftware the creators of the souls style games such as Bloodbourne, Dark souls and Demon souls. This game follows in the foot steps of these games with its difficulty and style of gameplay. This game was made in collaboration with fantasy novelist George R. R. Martin, who provided material for the game's setting hence the heavy medevil similar to dark souls but somehow very diffrent due to George R. R. Martains input.

It is out on 24th February 2022 if your a souls fan do not miss out on this stylistic challenging game


From one hard as nails medevil game to one where you can score headshots with some of the craziest weapons imaginable comes Tiny Tina's Wonderland, A spin off from the Borderlands series of games following fan favourite Tiny Tina. Slightly based of Borderlands 2 DLC assualt on dragon keep this game features a create a class in where out of the 6 main classes you can mix and match skills and abilitys to suit your gameplay style.

This game is looking on releasing in march 2022 and from the trailers seems to be the Borderland experience we needed taking the best parts from the previous 3 mainline games as well as the other spin offs to make one heck of a game!
